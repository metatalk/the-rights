"The Rights zorgt voor de correcte verdeling van auteursrechten tijdens evenementen"

Read

The Rights is a start-up located in the centre of Antwerp, co-working in the Telenet Idealabs Hub & currently participating in the Istart program in collaboration with Iminds. Our vision is to provide a single software platform which provides real-time data that:

  • Helps performance rights organisations to correctly allocate the fees they collected to artists whose repertoire has been played.
  • Gives artists the insights to oversee their own music.
  • Supports event organizers to facilitate the legally obliged administration.
